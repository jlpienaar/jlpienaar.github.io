Jacques Pienaar

Hi! I'm Jacques, a quantum physicist.

An Australian, I studied in Melbourne and received my PhD from the University of Queensland. My work has taken me all over the world; nowadays I'm based at the Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro, in Brazil.

In my research I try to make sense out of the notoriously confusing world of quantum mechanics. This blog is mainly a place where I can practice writing about this difficult problem for a general audience, and also a place for me to express thoughts that just don't fit in any academic journal -- because they are too small, too big, too unpolished, too tentative, too interdisciplinary, or maybe just too wild.

In my career I have studied many different interpretations of quantum theory. My current favourite interpretation is QBism and I work closely with the QBism group led by Prof. Chris Fuchs at the University of Massachusetts, Boston.
